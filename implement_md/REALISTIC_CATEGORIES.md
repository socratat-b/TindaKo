# Realistic Philippine Retail Categories

Based on SM Supermarket, Puregold, Robinsons Supermarket, Shopee/Lazada

## Food & Beverages

### 1. Beverages
- Softdrinks (Coke, Sprite, Royal)
- Juice drinks (Zest-O, Del Monte, C2)
- Bottled water
- Energy drinks (Cobra, Sting, Red Bull)
- Iced tea, milk tea

### 2. Instant Noodles
- Pancit canton (Lucky Me, Nissin, Payless)
- Cup noodles
- Instant mami/ramen
- Rice noodles (bihon)

### 3. Canned Goods
- Sardines (555, Ligo, Mega)
- Tuna (Century, 555)
- Corned beef (Argentina, Purefoods, CDO)
- Meatloaf, luncheon meat
- Vienna sausage

### 4. Coffee & Creamer
- 3-in-1 coffee (Nescafe, Great Taste, Kopiko)
- Black coffee
- Coffee creamer
- Milo, chocolate drinks

### 5. Condiments & Sauces
- Ketchup (UFC, Del Monte, Papa)
- Soy sauce (Datu Puti, Silver Swan)
- Vinegar
- Fish sauce (patis)
- Oyster sauce
- Hot sauce, chili sauce

### 6. Cooking Essentials
- Cooking oil (Minola, Baguio, Marca Pina)
- Salt, sugar
- Seasonings (Magic Sarap, Knorr cubes)
- Flour, cornstarch
- Spices

### 7. Rice & Grains
- Rice (Sinandomeng, Jasmine, Dinorado)
- Oats, cereals

### 8. Snacks
- Chips (Chippy, Piattos, Nova)
- Crackers (Skyflakes, Fita)
- Biscuits (Cream-O, Hansel)
- Cookies
- Nuts, popcorn

### 9. Candies & Sweets
- Hard candy (Kopiko, Mentos)
- Chocolate bars
- Gum, mints

### 10. Bread & Bakery
- Sliced bread (Gardenia, Neubake)
- Pandesal
- Pastries, cakes

### 11. Dairy Products
- Milk (Bear Brand, Alaska, Nido)
- Evaporated milk
- Condensed milk
- Cheese, butter
- Yogurt

### 12. Frozen Foods
- Ice cream (Selecta, Magnolia)
- Frozen meats
- Frozen vegetables

## Personal Care & Household

### 13. Bath & Body
- Bar soap (Safeguard, Palmolive)
- Body wash
- Shampoo (Pantene, Head & Shoulders, Rejoice)
- Conditioner

### 14. Oral Care
- Toothpaste (Colgate, Close Up)
- Toothbrush
- Mouthwash

### 15. Laundry & Cleaning
- Detergent powder/liquid (Tide, Surf, Breeze)
- Fabric conditioner (Downy)
- Bleach (Zonrox)
- Dishwashing liquid (Joy)
- Floor cleaner, toilet cleaner

### 16. Paper Products
- Toilet paper
- Tissue
- Napkins

### 17. Baby Products
- Diapers
- Baby powder
- Baby oil, lotion
- Baby food

### 18. Feminine Care
- Sanitary napkins
- Panty liners

### 19. Beauty & Cosmetics
- Face powder
- Lotion
- Deodorant
- Hair products

## Health & Others

### 20. Health & Wellness
- Vitamins
- Pain relievers (Biogesic, Neozep)
- First aid items

### 21. Household Items
- Light bulbs
- Batteries
- Candles, matches

### 22. Cigarettes & Tobacco
- Marlboro, Fortune, Hope, etc.

### 23. Load & E-pins
- Globe, Smart load cards
- Gaming pins
